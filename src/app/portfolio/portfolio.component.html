Aphlabet is one of the interesting projects I worked on last semester. Most of the Professors in Universities/Colleges use Google Classroom for their courses. The problem here is that :
  1. A lot of bandwidth is used to upload/download lectures, Assignments etc.
  2. Our Institute wants to come up with rules to allocate storage to the students.
To solve this problem we developed Aphlabet which is hosted locally on the Institute servers.
